The GiantSteps project aims to create the “seven-league boots” for music production in the next decade and beyond. We envision digital musical tools that unleash the creative potential of practitioners by targeting three directions: Developing and integrating musical expert agents, supportive and inspirational systems for melody, harmony, rhythm, structure or style, providing suggestions from sample to song level, while guiding users when they lack inspiration or technical or musical knowledge. These systems will be based on the latest findings in multimodal music information research (MIR) on symbolic, audio and metadata, as well as on new recommendation strategies
